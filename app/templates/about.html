Welcome to About BeerWell!

Overview


BeerWell is a tool to find the perfect match between person and beer.

It seems very possible that the best beer data comes from people's accounts of their experience drinking beer, e.g. text from beer reviews. I will try to use this data to better understand the beer world and recommend people beer.

Thanks to BeerAdvocate and all their users for the beer reviews used for this project! Thanks to Flask guy.

If you can make use of anything I have done here, please do :)
